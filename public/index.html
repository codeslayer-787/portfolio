About Me

I'm a Software Developer from Puerto Rico. I used to run a small company in the oil & gas industry, but was forced to close it down due to COVID-19. This gave me the oportunity to reflect about how I really want to live my life! When I was a child, I did not aspire to be an adult who makes lots of money. I used to tinker with my old Windows 98 computer (I've seen many blue screens of death). I would break it apart and put it back together. I also used to build and configure PCs for my friends just for fun. Someone once said "Choose a job that you love and you will never work a day in your life." That is the reason I decided to become a Software Developer! I'm an Air Force veteran as well as a police detective. I believe I can use my analytic skills to help solve problems in creative ways.
